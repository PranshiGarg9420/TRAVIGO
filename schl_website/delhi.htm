Humanyun Tomb

The tomb of Mughal Emperor Humayun, called Humayun’s Tomb, was built in Delhi in 1569-70. The construction of Humayun’s Tomb was commissioned by his first wife and chief consort Empress Bega Begum. This mesmerizing architecture is given true showcase Persian styles of Islamic architecture.

Akshardham Temple

Swaminarayan Akshardham Temple is one of the most important social, religious and cultural places of Delhi. It is inspired by Yogiji Maharaj.There are various exhibition halls created in the complex that displays an excellent sense of design and technology.

Lotus Temple

Lotus Temple is one of the most prominent attractions of Delhi. Known for its Lotus flower-like shape, the place is a Baha’i House of Worship. Construction of The Lotus Temple cost around $10 million. The temple is not just known for its scenic value. It is the serenity and calm of Lotus Temple that attracts people from across the globe.
